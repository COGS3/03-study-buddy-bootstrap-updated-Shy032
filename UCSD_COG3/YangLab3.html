Students learn 30% more when studying with Study Buddy



Study Overview

Problem: Students often find it difficult to find reliable study partners, leading to ineffective study sessions and poor performance.

Background: Research indicates that studying with peers can enhance learning, motivation, and retention of information.

Solution: The Study Buddy app connects students with compatible study partners, facilitating collaborative and effective study sessions.

Methods: The efficacy of the Study Buddy app was tested with randomized control trials (RCTs) involving 198 middle school students. Half of the students used the Study Buddy app while the other half studied alone.

Results: Students who used the Study Buddy app showed a significant improvement in their test scores, achieving 30% higher results compared to those who studied alone.
